Description

The Navy Office of Information presents the "Official U.S. Navy App" delivering up to date news and information for the Sailor, their families and the general public. View and then share stories, video and photos from around the fleet and learn why America's Navy is a warfighting-ready force, operating forward around the world.

Special features include: An interactive map to find key Navy installations and museums, special events, and the forward presence of our major carrier strike and amphibious ready groups. View the Navy Plan of the Day for important changes in pay and benefits, educational opportunities and uniforms, and check out the calendar for important dates for exams, selection boards, Navy outreach events, and more...
